Minerva does not necessarily prevent students from registering for courses that they should not take. Therefore, it is your responsibility to be aware of prerequisites, corequisites, restrictions, and faculty regulations that apply to the courses in which you register.

  1. Please click on HELP at the top right corner of your screen for instructions.
  2. If you receive an error message, click on it for detailed information.
  3. If you are unsure of which course sections to add, click Class Search to search and view the Class Schedule.

Satisfactory / Unsatisfactory grade option:

  • Please use the Grade Mode column to select Satisfactory / Unsatisfactory grade option.
  • Once you have submitted changes and the S/U Option appears under the Grade Mode column, this indicates that your changes have been saved. IMPORTANT - Verify all submissions to ensure against inadvertent changes to your Grade Mode option.
  • Please note that Freshman year (year 0) students in Arts, Science, and Agricultural and Environmental Sciences are not eligible to select the S/U option.

Waitlisted courses:

  • If you have added yourself to a waitlist, you may view your position on the waitlist on View Student Schedule by Course Section.
  • An email will be sent to you to invite you to register once a space is available but emails to notify you of your position as you move up the waitlist are no longer being sent.
